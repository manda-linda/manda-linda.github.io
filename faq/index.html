FAQ

What is the dress code?
Semi-formal meets cocktail. Linen suits. Nice dresses. Jackets. Tie optional. Join us to eat and dance away the night.
Covid precautions?
The event will take place outdoors under some large party tents. We expect our guests over the age of 5 to either be vaccinated or to wear a mask.

How do I get to the wedding venue?
Use your favorite maps app to find public transit directions. Otherwise, Lyft, or Uber are good options.
How to get around NYC?
Use your maps app to get subway and public transit directions. We highly recommend taking the subway whenever convenient. For those traveling with strollers see here for tips on navigating public transportation with strollers. Uber and Lyft also provide some options for carseats. Citbike is also a fun option if you are feeling adventerous.
Getting from the Airport?
JFK
If you are staying in Brooklyn, one of the quickest and most economical ways to get from the airport is via the LIRR to Atlantic Terminal. Check train times and directions in your maps app. Uber and Lyft also pickup from the airport. If you choose to take a yellow cab from the airport, you should prenegotiate your fare with the cab stand attendant. (Find price)
LGA
Uber, Lyft, or yellow cab are your best options.
EWR
Use your maps app for train times and directions.
